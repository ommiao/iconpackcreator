The custom mask has a strong effect on the uniformity of the icon pack, but it seems to be only suitable for regular icons. What kind of masks for irregular icons may not achieve good results. You can only rely on increasing the amount of adaptation.

Custom masks are actually very simple. What you don't do or do incorrectly is that you don't understand the basic principles of masks. The purpose of this tutorial is to make custom masks no longer a problem.

First of all, we must understand the meaning of the mask. The purpose of the mask is to make the icons without adaptation look more compatible with other icons, not to add some decorative effects to all the icons that have been adapted.

The mask effect is divided into three layers, from bottom to top are the background layer, the cropping layer of the original icon and the foreground layer, the effect of the three layers superimposed is the icon effect finally seen. The so-called background layer is to make a bottom. For example, if your icons are all round, then the background layer needs to be a circle of the same size; and the foreground layer is covered on the top, for example, you want to display a glass-reflective effect on the icon, you need to set the foreground into glass reflective material.

There is nothing in the background layer and foreground layer that need special introduction. The following focuses on the middle cropping layer.

Let's practice it below. The cropping layer is generally composed of black pixels and transparent pixels. Black represents the part of the original icon that needs to be cropped, and transparent represents the part of the original icon that needs to be retained. In addition, the cropping layer will be affected by the scale factor, we continue to test with a circular mask. The scale factor is a decimal, and the default is 1.0, which means no zooming. The significance of this parameter is that before cropping, the original icon is first zoomed, and then the zoomed icon is cropped. The zooming is because the original icon is different in size, and some may be cropped and some will not be cropped during cropping. After scale the cropping effect will be more uniform.
